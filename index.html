biryani

Prepare the Rice: Rinse and soak basmati rice, then cook it with whole spices until 70% done.Cook Vegetables: Sauté onions, ginger-garlic paste, and spices; add mixed vegetables and yogurt, cooking until tender.Layering: In a pot, layer the cooked vegetables and rice, topping each layer with fried onions, mint, coriander, saffron milk, and ghee. Dum Cooking: Seal the pot and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, allowing flavors to meld.Serve: Fluff the rice and serve hot with raita or a side salad. Enjoy!

Chana Masala

To prepare quick chana masala, boil soaked chickpeas until tender or use canned chickpeas. Sauté onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and spices (coriander, cumin, turmeric, garam masala) until fragrant, then add the chickpeas and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.

samosa

To prepare samosas, make a dough with flour, water, and oil, then fill with a spiced mixture of boiled potatoes, peas, and spices. Shape into triangles, deep fry until golden, and serve hot with chutney.
